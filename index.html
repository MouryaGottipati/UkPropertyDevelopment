About Us

UK Property Development has been created to combine the vast and wide ranging knowledge, experience and skills in sales, marketing, construction and property development. Along with a significant network of relationships and connections, creating one of UK’s most exciting new property development teams.

With experience working across the residential sector developing high-rise luxury city apartments, private and affordable housing, and student accommodation, we understand the expectations of city living and the value of the property marketplace. We work with private developers, local authorities and community agencies.
